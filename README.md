Stephen R. Donaldson's full **Chronicles of Thomas Covenant** — sorted not by power but by
**morality**: Hero, Anti-Hero, Grey — with the **Wielder and the Despiser** set apart as the
bound axis of all ten books. In the manner of the
[Bridge Burners](https://github.com/DavidWise01/bridge-burners) /
[Black Company](https://github.com/DavidWise01/black-company) /
[Wheel of Time](https://github.com/DavidWise01/wheel-of-time) /
[Gap Cycle](https://github.com/DavidWise01/gap-cycle) muster rolls.

> *You are mine.* — the Despiser, who is Covenant's own despair given a voice.

The Chronicles' hardest material is treated here as **archetype, not detail** — Covenant's
defining transgression is named because it is the engine of his guilt and his anti-heroism,
never depicted.

## The roll

### 🌿 Heroes — selfless servants of the Land
- **Lord Mhoram** — wisdom and compassion that refuse despair; the steady heart of the First Chronicles.
- **Saltheart Foamfollower** — Giant; Covenant's truest friend; joy, story, and the *caamora*; gives himself to the fire.
- **Pitchwife** — Giant of the Search; deformed and gentle; sings cracked stone whole.
- **The First of the Search** — Gossamer Glowlimn; Swordmain; iron, honor, long faith.
- **Sunder & Hollian** — Graveler and eh-Brand of the Sunbane; ordinary folk who carry the Land's hope forward.
- **Stave** — Haruchai cast out for choosing Linden over his people's pride.
- **The Ranyhyn** — the great horses of Ra; keeping promises across centuries.
- **Berek Halfhand** — Lord-Fatherer of legend; the lineage's headwaters.

### ⚖ Anti-Heroes — they save despite themselves
- **Linden Avery** — the Chosen; physician who Sees; saves through love and damns through it (raises the dead, wakes the Worm). The later Chronicles are hers.
- **High Lord Elena** — Covenant's daughter; breaks the Law of Death for love and vengeance.
- **Hile Troy** — the blind warmark who can see only in the Land; spends his very self for command.

### ▦ Greys — neither, or both
- **The Haruchai** — devotion curdled to pride; the Vow and the Mastery harm as much as they guard.
- **The Elohim** — beautiful, amoral powers; aid and obstruct by lights no mortal can read.
- **The Insequent** — the Mahdoubt, the Harrow, the Theomach; lore-masters who serve only their own purpose.
- **Kevin Landwaster** — the despair that loosed the Ritual of Desecration; the grey at the root of the grief.
- **Anele** — blind, mad earth-seer; truth wears him like a borrowed coat.
- **Esmer** — torn forever between aid and betrayal; grey made flesh.

### ◐ The Wielder & The Despiser — *set apart, the bound center*
- **Thomas Covenant** — the white gold wielder; the leper, the Unbeliever; guilty, and driven by that guilt; the wild magic that holds the Arch of Time answers his ring. The anti-hero on whom everything turns.
- **Lord Foul the Despiser** — the bitterness at the heart of the world; *"you are mine"* — Covenant's own despair given a voice, the destroyer who is also the proof of meaning.
